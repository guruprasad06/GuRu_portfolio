1. Smart city (Hardware Project)

This project is based on an Arduino microcontroller that automatically controls street lights using an LDR sensor. It saves energy by turning on lights only during low-light conditions and includes motion detection for smart illumination. Also it consists of tank water level indicator and Fire extinguisher system, making it a useful prototype.The software prototype is about the monotoring of palstic waste by cameras my using YOLO ML Concepts , where it distinguishes between plastic , dry waste , or paper , wood etc..

This is a hardware project where the bot was made using arduino , 5-array sensor,led's , motor driver and 12v battery. The biggest challenge was to callibrate the array sensor so taht it distinguishes the black and white line and travel accordingly..
